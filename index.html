Adidas Fashion Page

The Gucci Spring/Summer 2023 marks the arrival of a brand-new take on one of adidas’ most iconic runners – the ZX8000. Reimagined through Gucci’s contemporary lens, the signature silhouette is crafted in the House’s bright palette of suedes. Alongside the arrival of the archival look is the return of the instantly recognizable adidas x Gucci Gazelle, which blends adidas’ Three Stripes with the GG monogram. The latest footwear offering from the adidas x Gucci collection also includes two slip-on silhouettes – the Adilette and the Sideline – which both feature playful co-branded details and expressive color palettes.
